about us!

hello! this website is a combination of art and photography pieces by irene yoo, esther yang, and jessica leung.

irene, jessica, and esther are rising sophomores in high school. irene has a talent for art, creating elaborate pieces to express herself. esther likes to sketch and paint as a hobby, and creates various artwork during her spare time(: jessica has passion about photography, and sharing her unique experience with others. they hope to present their art by collaborating on this website.
